About Game

All characters of the game are fictional, any coincidences are random. Events Rolland Scopper unfold according to the film of the same name, and, therefore, the book of the same name :). Use any spells, as well as your knowledge and help Rolland get closer to solving another secret hiding within the walls of the school.
